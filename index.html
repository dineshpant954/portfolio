Featured · Agentic AI
Sales Pipeline Hygiene Agent
Dashboards surface problems — agents fix them. This agent ingests CRM pipeline data daily, detects stalled deals, missing fields, and stage anomalies, then auto-drafts Slack nudges to deal owners with full context and reasoning. No more deals slipping through the cracks because nobody noticed.

STEP 01
Ingest & Normalize
Agent pulls mock CRM/pipeline data — deals, stages, owners, amounts, last-touch dates — and normalizes it into a structured format for analysis.
STEP 02
Health Check & Flag
Runs daily anomaly detection: stale deals past stage benchmarks, missing required fields, deal size vs. engagement mismatch, ownership gaps.
STEP 03
Prioritized Action List
Generates a ranked list of deals needing attention with AI-generated reasoning: "Acme Corp $2.3M — 12 days in ETO review, avg is 5 days, no engineer assigned."
STEP 04
Auto-Draft & Report
Drafts nudge messages to deal owners and produces a weekly pipeline health report with risk scores — ready for human review before send.
